# Why I built this...

I spent weeks applying to "entry-level" roles and getting ghosted by machines. It felt like my resume was going into a black hole.

I started this project at 2 AM on a Tuesday because I was tired of "clean templates" that didn't actually help humans tell their stories. 

**My design philosophy here:**
- **Grain & Grit:** Life isn't perfect. I added the noise texture because the web is getting too "flat" and "generic."
- **Organic Shapes:** The blobs are intentional. Sharp corners are for corporate bots. 
- **The Workshop:** I wanted the home page to feel like my desk—messy, functional, and focused on building something real.

If you're reading this, I hope this tool helps you land that call. We need more humans in tech, not more numbers.

-- Aaditya
